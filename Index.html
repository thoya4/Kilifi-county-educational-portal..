kilifi County

Kilifi County is located on the coast of Kenya and is known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant culture, and rich biodiversity. The county is led by Governor Mung'aro, who is dedicated to promoting sustainable development and addressing climate change challenges. Moses Karema serves as the director of ICT in Kilifi County, overseeing technological initiatives to enhance education and communication.

About the Founder

Hi, I'm Sir Fredrick Kazungu, the founder of the Kilifi County Climate Education Portal. I established this website on May 10th, 2024, with the aim of providing accessible information and resources on climate change education to the residents of Kilifi County and beyond.

As a passionate advocate for environmental conservation and sustainable development, I believe that educating communities about climate change is crucial for building resilience and promoting positive environmental action.

Thank you for visiting our portal, and I hope you find the resources here informative and inspiring.
